Rustberry Supply

Branding Visual Identity Creative Direction Product Design

Rustberry Supply is about embracing adventure with a playful spirit. It's where curiosity meets creativity - a reminder that exploration doesn't always have to be serious or rugged. Rooted in earthy tones with pops of color, the brand celebrates spontaneity, friendship, and the joy of discovering new paths. Rustberry isn't about the destination, it's about carrying that spark of wonder wherever you go.

The Challenge

The challenge for Rustberry Supply was to forge a cohesive visual identity that seamlessly bridges two traditionally opposing worlds: the rugged, weathered resilience of the great outdoors and the sharp, minimalist aesthetic of modern urban streetwear. The primary hurdle lay in translating the brand's core philosophy - the intersection of "Rust" (symbolizing time, endurance, and raw material) and "Berry" (representing life, growth, and organic vibrancy) - into a singular design language. To achieve this, the branding had to move beyond the clunky, overly literal tropes of traditional outdoor gear. Instead, the project demanded a sophisticated system that feels equally at home on a technical mountain trail as it does in a high-end city boutique. This required a delicate balance of bold, heavy-duty typography paired with a refined, contemporary layout, ensuring the brand resonated with the "Modern Explorer" - a demographic that demands professional-grade durability without sacrificing a sleek, curated style. Finally, the identity needed to maintain complete versatility, remaining impactful across a diverse range of applications, from heavy-duty equipment labels to minimalist lifestyle apparel.
