KHAN

Branding Visual Identity Creative Direction Product Design

Khan is about reclaiming the power and grandeur of history with a bold, modern bite. It's where ancient tradition meets culinary craft - a reminder that eating can be an epic experience. Rooted in the legendary legacy of the Bulgarian khans, the brand celebrates strength, heritage, and the spirit of a conqueror. Khan isn't just about satisfying hunger; it's about honoring the past while fueling your own modern-day victories, one legendary burger at a time.

The Challenge

Creating KHAN meant building a historical passion into a large-scale, emotionally driven brand experience that stands out in a crowded market. My clients wanted to showcase Bulgarian history through their identity, which is why I developed a concept centered around the Khans - assigning a specific, unique burger to each historical leader. The goal was to create a powerful identity that unites tradition with modern culinary craft, proving that a burger isn't just a meal it's a taste of history.

The Outcome

The result is KHAN - a commanding brand identity that successfully bridges the gap between ancient legend and the modern fast-food landscape. By moving away from the generic, bright-colored tropes of typical fast-food chains, the brand establishes a "Premium-Aggressive" aesthetic that captures attention and dominates the street. KHAN now stands as more than just a burger joint; it is a tribute to a powerful legacy, proving that a strong, story-driven identity can outshine even the biggest global competitors.

Final Note

Ultimately, KHAN proves that a burger is more than just fast food-it is a vessel for storytelling. By merging the raw power of Bulgarian history with a bold, modern aesthetic, we've created a brand that doesn't just feed the hungry, it inspires the victor. KHAN is where tradition meets the street, delivering a "Ruthless Taste" that stays with you long after the last bite.
